Instructions

Riddle Riddle Riddle is divided into 3 categories:

English, Tagalog, and Mix.


The game has two modes:

Timed Mode - the player will be given 10 minutes to answer as many riddles as they can. In this mode, the player will not be game over even if he/she commits wrong answer. The game will be over if the player runs out of time.


Untimed Mode - the player will be given 3 lives. In every wrong answer the player will be deducted 1 life. The game will be over if the player consumed all his/her 3 lives.

            In Untimed, you have 3 hints to use. Those 3 hints will be recovered after every five riddles. The first hint is to show how many letters is the answer. The second hint is to show the first letter of the answer. And the third hint is to show what letter the answer ends.
